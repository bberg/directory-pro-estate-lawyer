## How to Compare Estate Attorneys

When weighing firms, look at:
- **Practice focus** - Does the firm specialize in your need (probate, trusts, elder law)?
- **Location** - Is the firm licensed and experienced in your state?
- **Services** - Wills, trusts, tax planning, asset protection, and administration.
- **Approach** - Personalized planning versus high-volume document preparation.
