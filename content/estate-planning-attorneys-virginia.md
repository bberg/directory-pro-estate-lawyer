## Frequently Asked Questions

### Does Virginia have an estate tax?
Virginia has no state estate or inheritance tax. Large estates may still be subject to the federal estate tax.

### Is probate required in Virginia?
Virginia requires probate to administer assets held solely in the decedent's name. The process is overseen by the Circuit Court and Commissioner of Accounts. Trusts and beneficiary designations can reduce probate.

### What is the difference between estate planning and elder law in Virginia?
Estate planning prepares for the transfer of assets, while elder law addresses aging-related issues like long-term care and Medicaid. Parks Zeigler handles both, which is useful for families planning for senior care.

### How do I choose a Virginia estate planning attorney?
Match the firm's location and focus to your needs, then contact a few for consultations to discuss your goals and fees.
